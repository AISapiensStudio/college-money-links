How to use this sheet (in 3 steps)

1) Start with the first 3 links

These cover the core aid forms and how aid is calculated. You’ll avoid surprises and gather the right documents early.

2) Add deadlines to your calendar

Most missed money is missed because of timing. The sheet guides you to the deadlines that matter (school + state).

3) Reduce costs before you borrow

Use credit-by-exam, tuition exchanges, and work benefits to lower the bill—then borrow only what you truly need.
